---
title: Livestreaming Custom Domain 
---

Use a custom domain for live streaming with Livepeer Studio.com

## Livepeer Ingest URL

The Livepeer Studio.com global-optimized ingest URL is
`rtmp://rtmp.livepeer.com/live/{stream-key}`.

## Naming your URL
- You can CNAME `rtmp.livepeer.com` to customize that URL to something with your
brand, e.g. `rtmp.companyname.io`. 

## Configuring your URL
- You can configure this with your DNS
provider.
